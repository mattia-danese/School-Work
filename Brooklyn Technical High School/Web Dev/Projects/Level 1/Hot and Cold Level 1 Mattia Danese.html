Aim of the Game

In order to win this game, you have to guess a randomly generated number, from 0-100, in 5 attempts or less.
To help you, you will be told the range of how far away each of your attempts are to the random number.
As a bonus, get "Very Hot" and you'll receive an extra attempt!
